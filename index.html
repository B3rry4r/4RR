Founder of the 4R Record Label

The 4RR is a Nigerian Record Label founded by Excel Erikefe in the year 2022. It is a musical label focused on discovering new artiste's of great talents in the country, artiste's who is in need of a little push to get them selves on the mainstream of today, many talents in our great country, Africa as a whole becoming populary known for our love for arts and especially our love for our music and our styles too. The label is set out for the discovery of many talents focusing the main genres being heard all over the world today, that would include our countrys main sound (AFROBEATS, HIP HOP, RNB, AND RARELY POP).
